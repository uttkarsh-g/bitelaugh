Hello everyone!

I'm thrilled to introduce you to Bite Laugh, a project aimed at bringing a local restaurant's menu online, with a primary focus on showcasing item prices.
This endeavor combines the meticulous design crafted in Figma with the robust development stack of HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, and GSAP.

Your feedback and contributions are highly valued! If you happen to spot any errors, please don't hesitate to commit the necessary changes.
Moreover, feel free to utilize this project for educational purposes, whether as a reference or for your college assignments.

Thank you for your support, and happy coding! ❤️
